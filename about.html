Who We Are

Soged is an educational app designed to preserve and teach Panama's Indigenous languages in a modern and accessible way. Our mission is to reconnect people with their roots and protect linguistic diversity for future generations.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Panama's indigenous languages through technology and education. We combine traditional knowledge with modern methods to make language learning accessible, engaging, and culturally respectful.

The Problem

Every two weeks, an indigenous language disappears. In Panama, many have been lost due to migration, discrimination, and lack of educational resources.

Of the 7,000 indigenous languages that exist, 40% are at risk of disappearing. The 2010 census showed a significant decline in speakers in Panama.

UNESCO and other organizations have launched campaigns to protect these languages, but more action is needed to ensure their survival.

Our Solution

User-Friendly App

Soged is a culturally respectful and easy-to-use app that makes learning indigenous languages accessible to everyone.

Community Engagement

We work with native speakers and communities to ensure authentic content and real impact.

Certification

We help users prepare for official certification, supporting the preservation and recognition of indigenous languages.
