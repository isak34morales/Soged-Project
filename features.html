What does Soged offer?

Discover all the features that make Soged a unique and powerful platform for learning and preserving Panama's indigenous languages.

Interactive Reading Lessons

Step-by-step vocabulary and reading activities to introduce you to each language in a fun and engaging way.

Native Speaker Audio

Listen to authentic pronunciation and practice with real audio from native speakers.

Writing Exercises

Strengthen your grammar and spelling with practical writing tasks and feedback.

Official Certification

Get certified as a native speaker through regional and university initiatives, and celebrate your progress.

Achievements & Badges

Earn badges and unlock achievements as you complete lessons and reach new milestones.

Progress Tracking

Track your XP, streaks, and lesson completion with a beautiful dashboard and performance manager.

Community Support

Join a vibrant community of learners and native speakers, and help preserve linguistic diversity together.
