Did you know that Panama has more than five active Indigenous languages, many at risk of disappearing?

Hi everyone! We are Soged, an educational app designed to preserve and teach Panama's Indigenous languages in a modern and accessible way.

Our Languages

Every two weeks, an indigenous language disappears. In Panama, many have been lost due to migration, discrimination, and lack of educational resources. Of the 7,000 indigenous languages, 40% are at risk of disappearing.

Ngäbe

The indigenous language with the highest number of speakers in Panama (54.9%).

  • Interactive lessons
  • Native audio
  • Certification available

Bokota

Only 0.4% of speakers, showing the urgency of preservation.

  • Writing exercises

  • Culture and traditions

What does Soged offer?

Official Certification

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Panama's indigenous languages through technology and education.

At Soged, we seek to reconnect users with their linguistic roots through local legends and cultural stories. We prepare speakers for certification, strengthening their grammar, reading, writing, and listening skills for better communication. Learn independently and flexibly, with lessons available anytime, both offline in the app and online on the website.
