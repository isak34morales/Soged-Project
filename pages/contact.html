Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform

How do I start learning indigenous languages?

Simply create an account and choose your preferred language. Our platform offers interactive lessons, cultural context, and native speaker audio to help you learn effectively.

Are the courses free?

We offer both free and premium courses. Basic lessons are free, while advanced courses and personalized tutoring require a subscription.

Can I practice with native speakers?

Yes! Our platform connects you with native speakers for conversation practice and cultural exchange sessions.

How accurate are the translations?

All translations are verified by native speakers and cultural experts to ensure accuracy and cultural sensitivity.
